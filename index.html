About

I like data that has consequences

Not dashboards for their own sake — models and reports that change what a team does on Monday morning.

I'm a data analyst and BI developer working across the full pipeline: pulling messy data out of source systems, cleaning and modeling it, and putting the result in front of people who need to act on it — whether that's a forecasting model behind an API or a dashboard a stakeholder checks every morning.

Most of my recent work sits at the intersection of classical statistics and applied machine learning: cohort and RFM analysis, ensemble models for prediction, and the SQL and DAX underneath it all.
